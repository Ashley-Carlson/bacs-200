Reasons to use this template

What is Roleplaying

Beutifully described by D20 PSFRD, A roleplaying game is an interactive story where one player, the Game Master (GM), sets the scene and presents challenges, while other players take the roles of player characters (PCs) and attempt to overcome those challenges." Thus, roleplaying is a great way to bond with friends as well as learn about yourself

Fat-free

Here at fennec fox technologies, we are committed to providing you a completely fat-free, all organic role playing experience. Our database is 100% ethically and naturally sourced from D20 PSFRD online databases here

Character Building

While time consuming, character creation can be a fun and exciting activity. The goal of this tool is to create a simplified compendium for new players and Dungeon Masters alike
