Steps required

I made this video with the following steps. Use these steps to record and publish a simple YouTube video from your phone.

  • Step 1 - Research and Create Presentation. As this is a complex subject requiring nuanced discussion of human behavior, an evidence based crisis prevention system was researched from the Crisis Prevention Institute was researched. Following this, the information on de-escilation and crisis prevention was added to a power point. It is impotant to note that the adoption of CPI in any institution requires contracting with the Crisis Prevention Institute. This video explains basic strategies to de-escilate a anxious client but does not provide appropriate training for a health care setting. I do not own, and am not affiliated with the Crisis Prevention Institute.
    To learn more about CPI and taking appropriate safety precausions, Click Here
  • Step 2 - Record. I used a Logitech C270 web camera and a Blue Yeti Microphone in order to record the video.
  • Step 3 - Edit. I used the Gallery to view all my videos and replay then for review.
  • Step 4 - Upload. I used the share to YouTube option to upload the video.
  • Step 5 - Publish. Let everything default in YouTube.
