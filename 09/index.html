Empower. Connect. Advocate.

In 2019, the united states accepted 30,000 asylum seekers and in 2019 it was estimated that about 28% of the United states population has immigrated to the united states in their lifetime. This has allowed many beautiful communities to flourish within our society and has expanded our economic growth over the years. However, growth from immigration has slowed and immigrant families face systematic persecution in their day to day lives. In order to fight back against this systematic persecution, the Immigrant Refugee Center of Northern Colorado has cultivated multicultural community that shares experiences and drives change.

Activities:

  • English Language Classes
  • Drivers Licence Training
  • Health and Census Day
  • "Lawyer Lady" in Office

Give Empowerment. Give Hope.

If you want to donate to this organization and advocate for those who have bravely traveled to the Home of the Free, please visit the the Immigrant Refugee Center of Northern Colorado website to see a detailed description of what your money can do and how you can help.
